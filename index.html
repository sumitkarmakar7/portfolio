Plasma Thruster Prototype (2024)

An experimental electric propulsion device demonstrating plasma physics principles through hands-on engineering implementation.

Technical Implementation:

  • Core Innovation: Designed compact high-voltage system converting 5V USB input to 10-15kV discharge capable of atmospheric plasma generation
  • Electrode Assembly: Fabricated concentric electrode geometry with adjustable spark gap (0.5-2mm) for plasma optimization
  • Power Management: Implemented voltage multiplier circuit with hand-wound ferrite-core transformer achieving 2000:1 step-up ratio
  • Performance Validation: Quantified plasma characteristics using optical analysis and high-speed videography (2000fps)
